The Annual Fundraising Gala is a special evening dedicated to raising funds and awareness for our education and healthcare programs. This event brings together donors, partners, volunteers, and community leaders to celebrate the impact we've made and to support future initiatives that transform lives.

This gala is more than just an event—it's a platform to bring people together for a shared purpose. Every contribution made during this evening helps us continue our mission of empowering communities and improving lives.
